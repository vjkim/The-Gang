Once upon a time, there was a group of shapes. They were mostly black and rectangular. They were mean and very serious.

So they kicked the 3 shapes that didn't conform out of their gang, which was called the Right Angle Gang.

And so these three shapes were sad, but found joy and comfort being around eachother. They soon started having a lot of fun.

They started rocking and rolling, being silly, and became the most well known shape gang in the universe.

They even started making fun of squares and rectangles in what they like to call 'Square Offs'!
